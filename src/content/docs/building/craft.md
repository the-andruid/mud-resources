Tools and references for designing rooms, areas, and player experiences in multi-user (MU*) games.

## Building

- [The Art of Building](https://www.addictmud.org/art_of_building/) - Resource for building rooms and areas
- [The Builder Academy](https://www.tbamud.com/) - Learn how to build your own world with tbaMUD, a MU* engine programmed in C
- [Grim Wheel's Buildcraft section](https://www.grimwheel.com/buildcraft/) - Repository of articles and advice on MUD building
- [Grim Wheel's Areas Archive](https://www.grimwheel.com/areas/) - Downloadable area files you can drop into your game
- [Writing room descriptions](https://writing-games.org/writing-room-descriptions-tips-and-examples/) - Tips and examples for evocative room writing

## Tools

- [MUD Colorizer Tool](https://coffee-nerd.github.io/MUD-Colorizer/) - Easily add color to text strings; supports {x, @x, $x, and other code styles

## Additional resources

- [MUDs.Fandom.com](https://muds.fandom.com/wiki/Main_Page) - Community wiki with a vast collection of game listings, articles, and resources, though many of them outdated
