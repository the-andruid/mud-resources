Hosting options for multi-user (MU*) games.

## Free / pay what you can

- [Vineyard.haus](https://vineyard.haus/) - Learn more about [Opie](https://writing-games.org/opie-giving-back-to-the-mud-community/), the sysadmin behind Vineyard, and why he offers free hosting

## Paid

- [PocketMUD](https://pocketmud.com/HostSplash/) (CoffeeMUD and now Evennia hosting, too)

Note: MUD admins have also had success with VM and cloud services such as Vultr, Digital Ocean, Linode, Google Cloud, and Microsoft Azure. For more ideas, check out [this list on Evennia's website](https://www.evennia.com/docs/latest/Setup/Online-Setup.html?highlight=remote#hosting-options-and-suggestions).
